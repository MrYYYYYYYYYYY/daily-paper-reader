## 摘要
可验证奖励的强化学习已成为提升大语言模型推理能力的核心工具，但当前方法在简洁性与信用分配之间存在权衡。GRPO等群组相对方法无需训练评论家，但通常为每个token分配轨迹级优势。演员-评论家方法提供更密集的学习信号，但需要学习值函数，其自身存在训练不稳定性。我们提出VIMPO，一种无需评论家的策略优化方法，该方法从KL正则化强化学习的最优性条件中推导出策略隐含的值函数。对于自回归生成，所得值递推关系可用策略-参考对数比表示，并由轨迹末尾无未来奖励的终止条件锚定。这给出一个简洁的值损失，无需训练评论家即可纳入结果级可验证奖励。相同的推导还可得到无评论家的演员优势，使VIMPO能够通过值损失分离奖励整合与通过PPO风格演员更新进行的策略改进。在数学RLVR基准测试中，VIMPO在MATH-500、AIME 2024、AIME 2025和OlympiadBench上均优于GRPO，在竞赛式评估中提升尤为显著。在含噪奖励下，VIMPO保持相对于GRPO的持续优势，表明策略隐含值优化可在保持无评论家训练实用简洁性的同时提供更精细的信用分配。

## Abstract
Reinforcement learning with verifiable rewards has become a central tool for improving the reasoning ability of large language models, but current methods face a trade-off between simplicity and credit assignment. Group-relative methods such as GRPO avoid training a critic, but typically assign a trajectory-level advantage to every token. Actor-critic methods provide denser learning signals, but require a learned value function with its own training instability. We introduce VIMPO, a critic-free policy optimization method that derives a policy-implied value function from the optimality conditions of KL-regularized reinforcement learning. For autoregressive generation, the resulting value recurrence can be written in terms of policy-reference log-ratios and anchored by the terminal condition that no future reward remains at the end of a trajectory. This gives a simple value loss that incorporates outcome-level verifiable rewards without training a critic. The same derivation also yields a critic-free actor advantage, allowing VIMPO to separate reward incorporation through the value loss from policy improvement through a PPO-style actor update. On mathematical RLVR benchmarks, VIMPO improves over GRPO across MATH-500, AIME 2024, AIME 2025, and OlympiadBench, with especially larger gains on competition-style evaluations. Under noisy rewards, VIMPO retains a consistent advantage over GRPO, suggesting that policy-implied value optimization can provide finer credit assignment while preserving the practical simplicity of critic-free training.

## 2. 论文提出的方法论：核心思想、关键技术细节、公式或算法流程
- **核心思想**：将自回归生成建模为确定性转移的MDP，利用KL正则化最优性条件推导出价值函数可由策略-参考对数比和终端边界条件隐式表示，从而避免训练显式评论家。
- **关键技术细节**：
  1. **自回归生成MDP形式化**：每个token前缀作为状态 \(s_t\)，确定性转移 \(P(s_{t+1}|s_t,a_t)=1\)，定义标准期望累计回报值函数 \(V(s_t)\)。
  2. **价值递归推导**：由KL正则化局部最优条件 \(\pi^*(\cdot|s_t) = \frac{\pi_{\text{ref}}(\cdot|s_t)}{Z(s_t)}\exp\left(\frac{1}{\beta}Q^*(s_t,\cdot)\right)\) 和贝尔曼方程 \(Q^*(s_t,a_t)=r(s_t,a_t)+\gamma V^*(s_{t+1})\) 可得核心恒等式：
     \[
     \beta\ln\frac{\pi^*(a_t|s_t)}{\pi_{\text{ref}}(a_t|s_t)} = r(s_t,a_t) + \gamma V^*(s_{t+1}) - V^*(s_t) + \beta\text{KL}^*(s_t)
     \]
     该式将log-ratio表示为贝尔曼残差加KL校正项。
  3. **策略隐含价值函数**：用可训练策略\(\pi\)替换最优策略\(\pi^*\)，并利用终端边界条件\(V(s_T)=0\)得到无评论家的价值损失：
     \[
     L_V(\pi) = \frac12\left( \sum_{k=0}^{T-1}\left[\beta\ln\frac{\pi(a_k|s_k)}{\pi_{\text{ref}}(a_k|s_k)} - \beta\text{sg}[\text{KL}_\pi(s_k)]\right] - (\bar{R}_{\text{final}} - \bar{R}_{\text{final}}) \right)^2
     \]
     其中sg为停止梯度，\(\bar{R}_{\text{final}}\)为群体平均奖励。该损失鼓励累积log-ratio跟踪中心化的最终奖励。
  4. **无评论家的演员优势**：相同推导给出闭合形式的一步TD优势：
     \[
     \hat{A}^{\text{TD}}_t = \beta\ln\frac{\pi_\theta(a_t|s_t)}{\pi_{\text{ref}}(a_t|s_t)} - \beta\text{KL}_{\pi_\theta}(s_t)
     \]
     可进一步用GAE形式累积多步优势，并经过标准化后用于PPO风格的演员损失\(L_A\)。
  5. **整体训练目标**：\(L_{\text{VIMPO}} = L_V + c_A L_A\)，其中外部分数奖励仅通过价值损失引入，演员更新使用策略内部的log-ratio信号，实现了奖励整合与策略改进的分离。
- **算法流程**（Algorithm 1）：
  - 每步采样prompt，从\(\pi_\theta\)生成G条轨迹；
  - 计算最终奖励及群体均值；
  - 对每条轨迹每个token计算\(\rho_t=\beta\log(\pi_\theta/\pi_{\text{ref}})\)和\(\kappa_t=\beta\text{KL}\)；
  - 定义隐含优势\(\hat{A}_t = \rho_t - \kappa_t\)；
  - 计算价值损失\(L_V\)（基于累积\(\rho_t - \text{sg}[\kappa_t]\)与中心化奖励的平方差）；
  - 标准化\(\hat{A}_t\)得到演员优势，计算PPO剪切演员损失；
  - 联合更新策略参数。
